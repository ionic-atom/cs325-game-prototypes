  • Gameplay: First the parent will boot up the game and go to Parental Set-up and choose the categories they want the child to go through. Next the child will then press Start, having 10 seconds per question. They will have three answers to choose from, they will just click one, the correct answer hopfully. At the end of the game there will be the final score to see if they won and recieve their reward. This game is meant to be played once a day, so rewards cannot become crazy.
